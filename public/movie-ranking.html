The Best Movies According To Emmanuel Taste

My Top Nollywood Movies From 2022-2023

Hey You

A shy nerdy guy meets a sexually confident woman via an adults-only website, but her life is complicated by the obsessive interest of other men.

Dinner at my Place

A Nigerian-American man is planning to propose to his girlfriend while she is having dinner at his place, however things take a turn for the worst when his ex-girlfriend unexpectedly shows up.

The Setup

Personal vendettas collide when a down-on-his-luck businessman hires a young female conartist to help with his scheme to marry a wealthy heiress.
